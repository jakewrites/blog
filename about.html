About Me

I'm Jake, a technical writer in South East England. I mostly write developer documentation and specialise in writing for translation.

I'm currently work for the IOTA foundation as a technical writer. The IOTA foundation is a non-profit organisation, building a protocol for the internet of things. This protocol will enable machines to communicate securely and send transactions among themselves.

I studied applied languages at the University of Portsmouth where I learned about translation (French and Spanish) and technical communication. This experience led me to become a technical writer, a role where I can merge my language and writing skills.

As well as languages and translation, I enjoy writing small programs that help me to automate day-today tasks. I'll write about these programs and my experience learning different technologies in my blog.
